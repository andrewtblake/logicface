Welcome to return of the beard/shades tester.

In this remake, you are presented with a logical expression, and must deduce its result.

As before, the first term will sometimes be "if", or sometimes "unless" -- i.e. the opposite of "if"

Click on each face in the image to toggle it from green to red and back.

In this way you need to construct the configuration of faces that you think the expression results in.

For example, if the expression is "if !(beard and shades)" you should make green all the faces except the one which has both beard and shades, which should be in red.

If you can get 20 correct and 0 wrong, you have probably grasped the concept, well done.
